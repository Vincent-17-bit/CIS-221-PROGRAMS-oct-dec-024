The Employee Management System

Select a module from the sidebar to view details.

Registration

The Registration module enables the addition of new employees to the system, capturing personal details, contact information, job roles, and other essential data. It ensures easy onboarding and accurate record-keeping for all employees.

Login

Module for employee login.

Finance

The Finance module helps manage payroll, employee compensation, bonuses, and deductions. It also assists with budget tracking, expense reporting, and financial analysis, ensuring smooth salary distribution and financial transparency within the organization.

Departments

The Departments module organizes employees into relevant departments, making it easier to manage teams, assign roles, and oversee department-specific activities. It also supports inter-departmental communication and collaboration within the organization.

Procurement

Module for handling procurement requests and tracking inventory.

Leave

The Leave module allows employees to submit leave requests, track available leave balances, and manage approvals. Managers can view schedules and make decisions that balance employee time-off requests with operational requirements.
